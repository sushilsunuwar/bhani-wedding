How we met

We first met during our MBBS days,2011. She was that cute, topper girl from Gandaki Boarding School,Pokhara and he was that good looking boy from Birendra Sainik Awasiya Mahavidyalaya, Bhaktapur,who everybody was talking about. We both have our versions of knowing each other.

I was quite impressed with his leadership, communication skills and the way he carried out every complicated task with ease during 1st year of college. I had never thought that I would ever interact with him,but destiny had other plans. Got chance to dance with him during our college dance competition, which we won. After that, during our community field posting,we started interacting, and I began to know the real Animesh and a true friendship started. I felt a strong connection, felt comfortable and introvert like me,started opening up,laughing and enjoying the moments with him.

My impression of Ms. Khanal was a shy, studious girl who used to sit in opposite side of the lecture hall during 1st year. During community visit, asked her for a dance for which she had said yes. Fast forwarding,being partner in dance competition to group mates in field visits,the bonding with her became strong. As a friend, she was always there for me in my highs and lows and a person who you can count on.

We were always in touch even after our MBBS days,and one day she popped out the question and the rest is history.
